About Me

Background

My name is Fabian Sugandhi, an eager and driven web developer based in Sydney, Australia. Coming from a Civil Engineering background, I have previously worked in the construction industry in both Estimating and Project Management side.

In 2024, having learned and experienced many aspects within the field of construction and contracting, it finally dawned on me that it was time to push myself to a new career direction, to finally venture somewhere I'm always passionate about. Armed with enthusiasm and determination, I decided to start my voyage into the IT industry as a web developer. And that journey started with my enrolment in Coder Academy.
